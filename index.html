About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Luxury Burger & Pastry has been serving exquisite cuisine in a warm and sophisticated atmosphere. Our passion for culinary excellence drives us to create memorable dining experiences for our guests.

Our executive chef, Abdurezak Tesfaye, brings over 20 years of experience from renowned restaurants across Ethiopia. His innovative approach to traditional recipes results in unique flavors that delight the senses.

At Luxury Burger & Pastry, we believe in using only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients to ensure freshness and support our community farmers.
